Detention Logs

We publish data, documents and investigations that reveal new perspectives on conditions and events inside Australia's immigration detention network. Detention Logs supports deeper community knowledge of and accountability for conditions in detention.

Data

So far Detention Logs has published 7632 incident reports produced between 3 Oct 2009 and 26 May 2011 in Australia's onshore detention facilities.

Investigations

The information on this website is used in reporting on and research into detention.
